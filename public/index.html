Multiplayer Hidden Word Card Game

Online room-code version with trays, dots, activity cards, and live scoring.

Start or Join

For real online play, this project must be hosted on a Node server. The HTML alone cannot sync players.

Rules Summary

Each player creates a 12-space tray using a secret word plus dots before and/or after it. Dots hide the true word length.

  • Slots 1–4 are worth 5 points each.
  • Slots 5–8 are worth 10 points each.
  • Slots 9–12 are worth 15 points each.
  • Draw an activity card at the start of each turn.
  • On your turn, ask one opponent for a letter or dot.
  • If they have more than one matching hidden space, they choose which one to expose.
  • A correct guess scores the exposed slot value and you keep guessing.
  • A miss ends your turn unless an additional-turn card is active.
  • Asking for a dot from someone with no hidden dot costs 50 points.
  • Interruptive guesses are available when an opponent has 5+ hidden spaces.
