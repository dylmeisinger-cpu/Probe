Rules Summary

Each player creates a 12-space tray using a secret word plus optional dots before and/or after it. Dots cannot be placed in the middle of a word, and unused spaces stay empty.

  • Tray scores by position are: 5, 5, 10, 15, 15, 10, 10, 15, 15, 10, 5, 5.
  • Draw an activity card at the start of each turn.
  • Ask one opponent for a letter or dot.
  • If they have more than one matching hidden space, they choose which one to expose.
  • A correct guess scores the exposed slot value and keeps your turn going.
  • A miss ends your turn unless an additional-turn card is active.
  • Asking for a dot from someone with no hidden dot costs 50 points.
  • Correctly exposing the final hidden space of a tray earns a 50-point bonus.
  • Interruptive guesses are available when an opponent has 5+ hidden spaces.
  • Optional category themes can give each round a prompt, like Medical or Animals.
  • Shared-device mode lets one computer host a tabletop/pass-and-play game.
  • Round-table UI mode puts players around a table with angled trays, while stacked mode keeps the original layout.
  • Players can upload small avatar pictures so their rack and score card are easier to recognize.
  • Click-to-expose mode lets players reveal cards verbally without typing the ask into the game.
